About Me

My name is Emanuel Joseph Vito, but I go by Joseph because my father shares the same first name. My father works as programmer for HealthFirst, a company that sells patient management software to medical institutions. My mother retired from 20 years of tech support to be a stay at home mother for my little sister. My brother is 3 years my junior and is currently trying to figure out what he wants to spend the rest of his life doing. My sister is a whopping 15 years younger than me, but I love her dearly and she is spoiled rotten.
